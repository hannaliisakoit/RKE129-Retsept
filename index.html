Caesar salad

Ingredient Amount
chicken fillet 2 pcs
a slice of bread 3 pcs
romaine lettuce 2 heads
grated Parmesan cheese â…“ cup (1 ounce)
caesar sauce as much as you like
  1. Cut the bread into cubes and bake in the oven until crispy.
  2. Fry the chicken fillet, season with salt and pepper and then cut into pieces.
  3. Cut the washed lettuce leaves into pieces and put them in a bowl, add the chicken pieces and Caesar sauce and mix.
  4. Sprinkle with bread cubes and Parmesan cheese and enjoy.
